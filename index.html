About Me

I am currently a 4th year Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. I work with Prof. Kai Chen in System networkING (SING) research group. I am also a recipient of Microsoft Research Asia Fellowship (2015). Before that, I received my B.E. degree in Information Security from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2013.

I will graduate from HKUST and join Cloud & Mobile Research Group at Microsoft Research Asia as an associate researcher in this summer.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in computer networking with a special focus on data center networking. I enjoy solving real world problems by building real systems. My Ph.D. thesis focuses on congestion control mechanisms for data center networks.
