Welcome to Jiawei Huang's Homepage!

I am a Professor (doctoral supervisor) and the director of Cyberspace Security Department in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Central South University, Changsha, China. He has secured more than 2.95 million RMB of research funding from National Natural Science Foundation of China. He has published more than 100 papers on a spectrum of leading conferences and journals, like IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking, IEEE Transaction on Computers, IEEE Transaction on Communications, IEEE INFOCOM, IEEE ICNP, etc. He has also applied for more than 30 national patents, 23 of which have been successfully granted.

Research: I work in the areas of cloud computing, data center networks, software-defined networks, machine learning, web optimization, video streaming and industrial Internet of Things.
