About Me

I am currently a 2nd year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology
I work with Prof. Kai Chen. I am a member of System networkING (SING) research group
Before that, I received B.E. degree in Information Security from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2013.

I am broadly interested in computer networking with a special focus on datacenter networking. I try to understand and improve network performance by building real systems. I enjoy solving real world problems in a simple and practical style (so-called 'dirty-state' works). But I don't like (also not good at) writing papers. Actually, I define myself as a junior network developer. I am lucky to work with my supervisor Kai Chen (who writes so many exciting papers for me) and outstanding collaborators: Haitao Wu from Microsoft Azure, Dongsu Han from KAIST, and Chen Tian from HUST.
